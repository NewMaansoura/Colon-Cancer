Understanding Colorectal Cancer

Colorectal cancer (CRC) starts in the colon or rectum. It often begins as small growths called polyps. While it is one of the most common cancers worldwide, it is also **one of the most preventable** through regular screening and early intervention.

Early Detection

90% survival rate if caught in early stages.

Lifestyle Risk

Diet and activity play a key role in prevention.
